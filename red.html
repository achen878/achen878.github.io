Red

The color of fire and blood. Associated with energy, war, danger, strength, power, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.



A very emotionally intense color. It enhances human metabolism, increases respiration rate, and raises blood pressure. It has very high visibility, which is why stop signs, stoplights, and fire equipment are usually painted red. In heraldry, red is used to indicate courage. It is a color found in many national flags.
